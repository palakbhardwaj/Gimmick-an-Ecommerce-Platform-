Origin Story

Once upon a time, there was a passionate gamer who loved to play games on his computer. He had a dream of building the ultimate gaming setup with the best peripherals available in the market. However, when he started researching online, he was shocked by the exorbitant prices of gaming peripherals.

Determined to find a way to build his dream gaming setup without breaking the bank, he started exploring local shops and found that they offered the same peripherals at much lower prices. It was then that he realized that many people like him were being scammed by online prices and missing out on great deals from local vendors.

With a desire to help fellow gamers and tech enthusiasts, he decided to start a website that would connect local vendors with buyers seeking affordable and reliable peripherals. Thus, the idea for his website was born, and he set out to make his vision a reality. Today, his website is a go-to destination for those looking to buy quality peripherals at a fair price from trusted local vendors.
